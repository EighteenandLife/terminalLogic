Welcome to terminalLogic

This is my playground for researching financial markets. In short, terminalLogic is a 300GB virtual computer hosted on AWS. Its primary function is to operate as an algorythmic derivitives trading program that hedges on unsupervised machine learning libraries to detect conditions present in Bitcoin derivitives markets and place speculative bets based on the probablitiy of profiting.

In theory markets exist in one of a few states, terminalLogic identifies those states and makes trading decisions if there is enough edge. I have built out two different algorythims that each trade a specific strategy during favorable market-state conditions for that particualy strategy. Each strategy performs poorly on their own but when selectivly traded, the combined results are a remarkable improvement. terminalLogics use of SKLearn's Machine Learning libraries produce a signal for the market state. If the market is in a mean-reverting(ranging) state, or if the market is trending(going up, going down). The ML library produces a cluster of market states, each cluster has different mean rolling price averages and covariance. terminalLogic scores the regimes and sends a signal to the decision engine. The decision engine looks at the last 20 minute period of market data and backtests both trading strategies and produces a score for each strategies performance. If the ML library and the back test for the last period agree on the market state, a note is logged and that same algo is implimented for the next period (assuming a ranging market will continue to range etc).
